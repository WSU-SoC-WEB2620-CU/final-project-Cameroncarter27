About Me

Hi, I’m Cameron and for as long as I can remember I have loved going to arcades! They have always had a very special place in my heart as they have been my chosen spot for several birthdays, hang out sessions with friends, dates, or fun evenings with my family. I would say I was always a fan of arcades but my increased interest in them came in the seventh grade during a field trip. While on this field trip I noticed that the arcade we were at had a Crossy Road machine which I had had on my phone for awhile and was a really big fan of so I decided to play it. Well the arcade version works by having you try to beat a certain score which then gives you a jackpot and on this trip I kept getting really close. I knew I could beat the score if I just put more time into the game and so that’s exactly what I did. Every time I came back to that arcade I would immediately go to Crossy Road. I started winning more and more consistently and getting all-time high score after all-time high score until I eventually saved up 65,000 tickets and won a Nintendo Switch. This made my interest in the arcade sky-rocket as I now knew if I just put in the time and effort I could win the big prizes that I had always dreamed of getting when I was younger. So I kept playing and playing and winning more and more until I won an X Box and then a Playstation and then two more X Boxes and two more Switches etc. Now, I’m looking to help others do what I do and that’s exactly why I made this website, to give guides to people that allow them to improve their skills at certain arcade games and win those big prizes that have been begging to be won for so long!
